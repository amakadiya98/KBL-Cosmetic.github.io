Company Profile

FACILITY SIZE Approx. 80,000 square feet.
LOCATION 475 N. Sheridan St. Corona, CA. 92880, near the intersection of the 91 and the 15 freeway.
TEAM 80 – 120 Associates based on Production Requirements.
PRODUCT SPECIALTIES Anti-Aging Lotions, Creams, Serums, AHA/BHA, Stabilized Vitamin C, Baby Care Products, Bath & Body Products such as Body Washes, Shower Gels, Body Lotions, Face Masks, and Body Scrubs. Professional Salon Products, Natural Sunscreens, Hand Sanitizing Gel, Acne Treatment Products, Liquid Supplements, Amenities Lines, Scrubs, Masks, Toners, Facial Cleansers, Men’s Skin Care, Pet Care, and many more.
MANUFACTURING & CAPABILITIES 18 stainless steel, steam heated tanks with sizes from 400 – 23,500 lbs.
FILLING EQUIPMENT Nine (9) permanent filling lines plus two (2) portable lines including: variety of recirculating pressure fillers, automatic/semiautomatic piston fillers, and multiple tube sealers, hot fills, induction sealing, shrink wrap and tampered proof sealing. Bottom coding, automatic cappers, automatic labeling equipment and automatic carton sealers.
LABELING EQUIPMENT We can service; front, back and wrap around labels. Position 4 Double sided. (Front position 4 – Back position 3)
TECHNICAL SERVICE Experienced Chemists and Technicians with over 50 years of personal care formulating and product development experience. Experience in all phases of Research and Development, Quality Control, FDA licensed to manufacture OTC Products.
MACHINE SHOP On-site repairs and equipment modification. Fabricate tooling. Mechanics on site.
SHIPPING Three loading docks, three additional loading doors. UPS, FEDEX, common carriers or containers. Experienced in Intl/Export in handling all documentation, BOL and Custom requirements. Easy access to the shipping ports.
